FAQ

Security Questions Answered

Everything your security and compliance team needs to know.

How does AI Shield protect our company data? +
AI Shield monitors text in real-time as employees type in AI platforms. When sensitive data is detected (NIF, credit cards, IBANs, personal IDs, etc.), it triggers an immediate visual alert before the data is sent. All detections are logged for compliance auditing, with data masked (e.g., "123456789" becomes "***456***") to maintain privacy.
What European data types are protected? +
We protect 15+ European identifiers: NIF (Portugal), NIE and DNI (Spain), INSEE (France), National Insurance and NHS numbers (UK), Codice Fiscale (Italy), BSN (Netherlands), Steuer-ID and Sozialversicherungsnummer (Germany), plus IBAN, VAT numbers, passports, and EHIC cards. We also detect global data types like credit cards, emails, and phone numbers.
Is AI Shield truly GDPR compliant? +
Yes. AI Shield is designed specifically for GDPR compliance. We use data minimization (only masked data stored), maintain complete audit trails, provide data subject access reports, and help organizations demonstrate compliance to regulators. Our architecture follows Article 25 (data protection by design) and Article 32 (security of processing) requirements.
Where is sensitive data stored? +
Sensitive data never leaves the user's browser in plain text. Only masked/hashed versions are stored in our EU-based servers for audit purposes. For example, a NIF "123456789" is stored as "***456***". Full data is not accessible to our systems or staff. You maintain complete control and can request deletion at any time under GDPR Article 17.
How accurate is the detection? +
AI Shield achieves 99.7% accuracy on structured data types (national IDs, credit cards, IBANs). We use two confidence levels: RED alerts for confirmed threats (high-precision patterns with checksum validation) and YELLOW alerts for suspicious patterns (emails, keywords). This two-tier system minimizes false positives while maintaining comprehensive protection.
Can we customize detection rules? +
Enterprise customers can define custom patterns and sensitivity levels. For example, you can add proprietary document IDs, internal reference numbers, or industry-specific identifiers. Contact our security team to discuss custom detection requirements for your organization.
What happens during a security audit? +
AI Shield provides complete, exportable audit logs showing all detected data leaks, user activity, and prevention actions. Reports include timestamps, data types, user identifiers (anonymized if required), and AI platforms accessed. These reports are designed to satisfy GDPR Article 30 (records of processing), ISO 27001, and SOC 2 audit requirements.
How quickly can we deploy AI Shield? +
Deployment takes approximately 2 minutes per user. Install the Chrome extension, configure with your company API key, and protection is active immediately. For enterprise deployments, we can pre-configure and distribute extensions via Chrome Web Store or internal app stores. No infrastructure changes or IT involvement required.
