// FAQ

Questions we actually get asked.

Do I need to involve my IT department to install it?
No. AI Shield is a Chrome extension — any employee can install it themselves in under 2 minutes. For company-wide deployment, you can push it silently via Google Workspace Admin without touching a single employee's machine manually.
Does my employees' data pass through your servers?
Never. All scanning and detection happens locally inside the browser extension. The content of your employees' prompts, files, or clipboard data never leaves their device and is never sent to AI Shield's servers.
Does it work on Firefox or Edge?
AI Shield currently runs on Chrome and all Chromium-based browsers, including Microsoft Edge, Brave, and Arc. Firefox support is on our roadmap for Q3 2026. Most enterprise environments run Chromium-based browsers, so coverage is typically complete.
Can I trial it without connecting my Google Workspace?
Absolutely. Individual employees can install the extension directly from the Chrome Web Store and start the trial immediately — no admin access, no Google Workspace connection required. The Workspace integration is only needed for silent company-wide deployment.
How does it help with GDPR Article 32 compliance?
GDPR Article 32 requires "appropriate technical measures" to protect personal data. AI Shield provides documented evidence of those measures: every detected attempt to share personal data with an AI tool is logged, timestamped, and exportable as an audit report for your DPA.
What's the minimum commitment? Is there a lock-in?
No lock-in. All plans are billed monthly and you can cancel at any time. We offer annual billing at a discount if you want it, but it's never required. The 14-day free trial requires no credit card and cancels automatically if you don't upgrade.
Can AI Shield replace our existing DLP solution?
AI Shield is purpose-built for AI tool monitoring — the blind spot that traditional DLP solutions weren't designed for. It works well alongside your existing DLP for complete coverage. For SMBs without a DLP solution, AI Shield handles the most critical risk vector at a fraction of enterprise DLP costs.
What happens when AI Shield detects sensitive data?
The employee sees a clear, non-alarming alert explaining what was detected and why it's risky. They can choose to remove the sensitive content and continue, or cancel the action. Admins receive a notification and the event is logged in the dashboard with full context for coaching or reporting.
