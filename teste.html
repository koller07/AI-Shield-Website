// Capabilities

Every layer of protection your compliance team needs.

Built specifically for the AI era — not a legacy DLP patched to support ChatGPT.

Real-Time Detection

Instant alerts the moment an employee attempts to share CPF, CNPJ, IBAN, credit cards, API keys, or confidential contracts. Blocking happens before the data ever reaches the AI platform.

```

Context-Aware AI Engine

Unlike legacy DLP tools that match patterns and flood you with false positives, AI Shield understands context. It knows the difference between "Client IBAN" and "example IBAN in a tutorial".

Human Firewall Coaching

Instead of silently blocking, AI Shield coaches employees in the moment — explaining what was detected and why. Builds a security-aware culture rather than just adding friction.

Audit-Ready Compliance Reports

Export detailed logs for regulators at any time. Every detection is timestamped, categorised by data type, and linked to the employee and platform. Your DPO will thank you.

Zero Data Storage

All scanning happens locally in the browser. AI Shield never sees, stores or transmits your employees' data to our servers. Privacy-by-design from the ground up.

Slack & Teams Integrations

Get detection alerts directly in Slack or Microsoft Teams. Admins receive instant notifications when high-risk events occur, without needing to check the dashboard constantly.
